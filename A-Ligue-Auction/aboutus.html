This was developed in 2015 to bring out the best in students A-Ligue (A for Anna University and Ligue is a French word of League) The Alumni and College Football Players of Anna University (CEG & AC Tech) initiated a competitive and fun football tournament called the A-Ligue in 2013. This tournament which was inaugurated on the eve of Jan 26, 2013 became an opportunity to identify young footballing talents of Anna University and also provide more match experience to the current college team players. A-Ligue started with 5 teams in 2013:

  • Dribbling Demons
  • Faking Phantoms
  • Juggling Giants
  • Netbusters
  • Soccer Hooligans
As it grew bigger, a team from Madras Institute of Technology joined the A-Ligue in 2018.
